Welcome to the University of Ilocos Philippines Admission Page.

Admission Requirements

Submit Original Copies plus 3 sets of photocopies.

High School Graduate (2015 and Below)

  • High School Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate issued by PSA
  • 2 pcs. passport size photo (white background, with name tag)

Senior High School Graduate (2016 and Above)

  • SF 9 (Senior High School Report Card)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate issued by PSA
  • 2 pcs. passport size photo (white background, with name tag)

ALS/A&E Passers (Secondary Level)

  • ALS Rating (PASSED)
  • Certificate indicating eligibility for college admission
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate issued by PSA
  • 2 pcs. passport size photo (white background, with name tag)

Transferees (In-bound)

  • Transfer Credentials
  • Certificate of Final Grades
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Birth Certificate issued by PSA
  • 2 pcs. passport size photo (white background, with name tag)

Cross-Enrollee (In-bound)

  • Permit to Cross-enroll

Returning Students

  • Certificate of Final Grades or class cards from the previous semester

Graduate School

  • Transfer Certificate
  • Certificate of General Weighted Average (GWA)
  • Official Transcript of Record
  • Birth Certificate or Marriage Contract (if married) issued by PSA
  • 2 pcs. passport size photo (white background, with name tag)
